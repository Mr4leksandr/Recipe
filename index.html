PEPPERONI PIZZA

Recipe from readyseteat.com

INGREDIENTS

Hunt's® Tomato Sauce-No Salt Added 1 can (8 oz each)
Grated Parmesan cheese 1/4 cup
Dried oregano 1/4 teaspoon
1 prebaked thin pizza crust 12-inch
Shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese 1 cup
Sliced pepperoni 1/3 cup

NUTRITION INFORMATION

242 calories, 27g Carbs

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F. Combine tomato sauce, Parmesan cheese and oregano in small bowl; spread tomato sauce evenly over crust. Sprinkle pizza with mozzarella cheese; top with pepperoni.
  2. Place on ungreased pizza pan. Bake 12 to 15 minutes or until edges of crust are browned lightly and cheese melts. Cut pizza into 6 slices.
